My Story

Hello there! I'm Rakshith Suresh, a passionate data analyst dedicated to solving real-world problems through data insights. Outside of work, I enjoy exploring the outdoors and capturing life's moments with my camera.


Bit more about me

Born and raised in Mysore, India, my fascination with technology and analytics led me to pursue a Master's degree in Information Systems at California State University, Long Beach. Professionally, I have over two years of experience working as a cloud engineer at a multinational corporation, where I honed my technical skills and contributed to significant projects. Outside of work, I immerse myself in a variety of hobbies: photography, capturing the unnoticed beauty around me; hiking, to reconnect with nature; and maintaining my fitness through gym workouts and regular runs. I enjoy cooking, watching sports, and spending quality time with friends and family. Passionate about data visualization with tools like Tableau and Power BI, and skilled in SQL and machine learning, I am committed to making complex data accessible and insightful. My wanderlust drives me to explore every national park in the United States.
